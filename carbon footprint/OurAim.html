Keeping in mind the increased use of technology in our daily life we aim to take the steps in order to save our environment and future. We aim to provide you the estimation of how much C02 you use in your day to day life.


 The Technological Carbon Footprint calculator collects your data on different basis. This includes your time on spend on searching the web, sending mails, watching videos etc. By using a simple algorithm the calculator estimates how much carbon footprint you generate.
